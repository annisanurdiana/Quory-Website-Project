About QUORY

Quory is derived from the words "Question or Reply." Quory is a public platform where anybody may ask questions or answer questions. This platform's name is derived from database query programming. A request for information is referred to as a query. 

Therefore, it is hoped that the Quory platform will be present as the essential aspect of the discussion forum, addressing all human needs. We can ask questions or answer inquiries to aid others with their doubts and life problems.

So what are you waiting for? Register yourself by clicking  Sign Up , if you are already registered, you can click  Log In  to enter this website. Enjoy your freedom in sharing information with others and make new friends with an educated background.
